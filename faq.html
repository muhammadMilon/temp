Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI personalize my roadmap?

We use Gemini to compare your skills with your target career path, identify gaps, and generate milestones. As you complete quizzes and rate milestones, the roadmap adjusts.

Where do course recommendations come from?

AI suggests courses from Coursera, Udemy, YouTube, and other sources based on your current milestone and skill level.

Is SkillVoyager.AI free?

Core features including roadmap generation and progress tracking are free. Premium features may be introduced later.

Can I change my career goal?

Yes. Update your goal in Profile & Settings. A new roadmap will be generated based on the change.
