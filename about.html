About SkillVoyager.AI

SkillVoyager.AI is an AI-powered learning roadmap platform that helps you plan and achieve career-aligned skill goals. Using the Gemini API, we analyze your current skills, identify gaps, and generate personalized, adaptive roadmaps with milestones, course recommendations, and progress tracking.

Our Mission

To make learning efficient, personalized, and goal-oriented. Unlike static roadmap sites, we adapt to your progress and feedback.
